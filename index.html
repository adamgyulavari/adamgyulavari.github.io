Projects

Besides work I like to work on small side projects or for NGOs, here are a few them that are still running.

Konnektlive

Konnekt is a Hungarian NGO with the purpose to help high school students in various ways. I've participated in mentoring and organizing events. I've created this website for the event called Konnekt Live where the students can meet with many different professions and can have a conversation about what that job looks like on a regular day. So they can get an idea of the profession and decide where to go after high school. The website uses Ruby and Sinatra for the backend and a little vanilla JavaScript on the frontend.

Mixtape

Mixtape is a community playlist, where you can log in with your Spotify account. Create a playlist from your top 20 songs from Spotify and then you can share the playlist with others so their top 20 songs are added to the playlist. Made with Ruby on Rails (and love :D).

Polly

We used Slack at GFA with many students and the Simple poll extension was really great until they put a price on it (and with ~1000 slack users we didn't fit in the free tier). So I've created a simple clone with nodejs.
